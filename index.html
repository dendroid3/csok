Computer Society of Kimathi

Build The Future. Hack The Present

We are an academic-technology club founded in 2012 that has a mission of cultivating a tech structure through meetups, talks, training, hackathons and social activities for its members.

The club membership is open for students and staff within the university. We aim to create awareness, promote growth of computer knowledge and entrepreneurship and nurture technological and programming talent.

About CSoK

CSoK connects driven developers, designers, and problem-solvers from Dedan Kimathi University to collaborate on real projects and technical growth.

We host workshops, build nights, hackathons, and mentorship tracks that move members from curiosity to real-world impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT_IS_CSOK?

We are an academic-technology community founded in 2012, dedicated to cultivating a robust tech structure through meetups, workshops, and collaborative project development.

WHO_IS_ELIGIBLE_FOR_ACCESS?

Membership is open to all students and staff within the university who are interested in promoting the growth of computer knowledge and entrepreneurship.

HOW_DO_I_INITIATE_MEMBERSHIP?

Navigate to the \"Join Now\" uplink and complete the registration sequence to be added to our active node.

WHAT_ACTIVITIES_DO_YOU_EXECUTE?

We host practical workshops, guest lectures, hackathons, and social activities designed to nurture technical and programming talent.

ARE_THERE_INDUSTRY_COLLABORATIONS?

Yes, we actively collaborate with hubs (like DeHUB) and industry partners to share ideas and develop them into viable products.

HOW_DO_I_TRACK_UPCOMING_OPERATIONS?

Check the \"Upcoming Events\" feed on the main dashboard for schedules on technical talks and professional development sessions.

CAN_I_CONTRIBUTE_TO_PROJECTS?

Absolutely. We encourage collaboration across all schools to turn conceptual ideas into reality within our tech community.

DO_I_NEED_PRIOR_TECHNICAL_SKILLS?

No. CSoK is designed to promote the development of knowledge into technical skills; beginners and experts are both welcome to join the mission.

WHO_MANAGES_THE_SOCIETY?

The society is directed by a team of student leaders ranging from AI/ML engineers to Cybersecurity and Blockchain experts.

HOW_DO_I_CONTACT_OPERATIONS?

You can reach out via our official social media channels linked at the bottom of the interface.
